TimberSim

Timber transportation simulation

TimberSim models Mississippi’s timber distribution network using discrete-event simulation to optimize truck routing, crane scheduling, and resource utilization. It evaluates breakdown impacts, queues, travel times, and rerouting policies to cut delays and boost throughput. The project is funded by USDA.
